# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume positive intent.
- Focus feedback on code and outcomes, not people.
- Welcome different perspectives and experience levels.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, or personal attacks
- Trolling, insulting, or derogatory comments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated disruptive behavior in discussions or reviews

## Enforcement

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code.
They may remove comments, reject contributions, or limit participation for violations.

## Reporting

Report conduct issues privately to:

- conduct contact: `conduct@gaialynk.com`

All reports will be reviewed and handled confidentially as much as possible.
